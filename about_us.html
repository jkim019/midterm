Our Promises

We promise to curate the most wholesome smoothies to help our customers improve their body and mind. We source sustainably & ethically-sourced farm-fresh, organic, and above all, tasty ingredients for our smoothies. Our customers can be sure that all of our smoothies are delicious and nutritious fuel for the bodies. We spread a message of self-love and discovery as health and fitness is should be viewed from a holistic approach. We strive for a community built around building each other up.

Our Mission

We are proud to be a carbon-neutral company. This is posisble due to our use of local ingredients which take less resources to transport and our commitment to using the most fuel-efficent machinery.

Our Commitment

We pledge to donate 5% of our profits to the National Forest Foundation. Each and every smoothie you buy is a step towards combatting climate change and keeping the Earth we live on happy and healthy.
